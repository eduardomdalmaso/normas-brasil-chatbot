(See Appendix D to 29 CFR 1910.146, Sample Permits.) The duration of entry permits must not exceed the time required to complete an assignment. Also, the entry supervisor must terminate entry and cancel permits when an assignment has been completed or when conditions that are not allowed arise. New conditions must be noted on the canceled permit and used in revising the permit space program. The standard also requires the employer to keep all canceled entry permits for at least one year. Upon completing this training, employers must ensure that employees have acquired the under- standing, knowledge and skills necessary for the safe performance of their duties.
