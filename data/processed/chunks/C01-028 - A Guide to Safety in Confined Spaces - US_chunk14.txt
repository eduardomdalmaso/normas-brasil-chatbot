Training is also required for rescue team members, including cardiopulmonary resuscitation and first-aid training (see Emergencies). Employers must certify that training has been accomplished. Upon completion of training, employees must receive a certificate of training that includes the employee’s name, sig- nature or initials of trainer(s), and dates of training. The certification must be made available for inspection by employees and their authorized representatives. In addition, the employer also must ensure that employees are trained in their assigned duties.
